Why I started this blog

blognoteslearningtech

This is my little corner of the internet to share what I’m learning as I transition from Automation Engineering into DevOps.

I’ve been meaning to write publicly for years. The usual excuses apply — not enough time, nothing interesting to say, someone else already wrote it better. But the truth is, writing is how I think. And thinking out loud, even imperfectly, is more useful than staying quiet.

What this will be

Mostly things I learn along the way. If I solve a problem, I’ll write about it. If I build something, I’ll share how and why I did it. And if I come across something that changes how I think, I’ll probably write about that too.

No strict niche. No posting schedule. Just useful things worth documenting.

Why now

I got tired of forgetting things I’d already figured out. A fix that took hours to troubleshoot. A config that finally worked after days of trial and error. Six months later, gone.

Writing things down helps me learn better and keep track of what I’ve learned. And if someone else finds it useful too, even better.

If you’re reading this — welcome. More soon.
